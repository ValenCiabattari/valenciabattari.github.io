UTV Competition Vehicle Preliminary Design

Preliminary design of a competition UTV focused on vehicle layout, front suspension kinematics, safety cage development, and CAD integration under FIA-oriented design constraints.

FDC Diseño y Desarrollo | 2025 – Present

Overview

This project involved the preliminary development of a competition UTV concept, with emphasis on technical packaging, mechanical architecture, suspension definition, and structural safety considerations. The work was carried out as part of a two-person team, under the guidance of experienced mechanical engineers within the company.

My Role

My contribution focused on the early-stage engineering definition of the vehicle, especially in areas related to design integration and suspension layout. This included positioning key vehicle elements, developing the safety cage concept under FIA constraints, and working on the front suspension geometry through kinematic analysis tools.

Key Contributions

  • Defined the preliminary layout and packaging of major mechanical and structural vehicle elements.
  • Designed the safety cage following FIA safety requirements and geometric constraints.
  • Worked on front suspension kinematics using OptimumKinematics to support the definition of suspension pickup points.
  • Studied steering system positioning in relation to suspension geometry and front-end packaging.
  • Managed and developed large CAD assemblies in SolidWorks for integrated vehicle design.
  • Contributed to concept benchmarking and technical decision-making during the development process.

Engineering Focus

One of the most valuable parts of the project was the suspension study. The front suspension concept was analyzed with OptimumKinematics in order to define feasible hard-point locations and evaluate how suspension arms and steering components could be positioned within the available package. This work helped connect theoretical vehicle dynamics concepts with practical design decisions.

Tools Used

SolidWorks OptimumKinematics Vehicle Packaging Suspension Kinematics Safety Cage Design FIA Constraints

Project Value

Beyond the technical outcome, this project was an important learning experience in real vehicle design, collaborative engineering work, and system-level integration. It strengthened my understanding of how suspension, structure, packaging, and safety constraints interact during the preliminary design phase of a competition vehicle.
