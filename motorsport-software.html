Motorsport Technical Management Software

FDC Diseño y Desarrollo | 2025 – Present

Internal engineering software platform developed for motorsport applications, designed to centralize technical information and improve the analysis of vehicle set-up, tyre usage, aerodynamic configurations, track sessions, and circuit-related data.

  • Developed a Python-based desktop application with graphical interface and integrated database architecture.
  • Implemented modules for set-up management, tyre history, aerodynamic parameters, event/session organization, and lap sector analysis.
  • Improved engineering workflow efficiency by making competition data easier to store, retrieve, compare, and interpret.
